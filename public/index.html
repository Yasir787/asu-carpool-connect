🚗 Our Story

ASU Carpool Connect was created to solve a simple problem: many students and staff at Arizona State University travel similar routes but often travel alone. Carpooling benefits everyone—it reduces costs, saves time, and creates a stronger university community.

Why Carpool at ASU?
  • 💰 Save Money — Split gas, tolls, and parking costs
  • 🌍 Reduce Emissions — Drive alone, or ride together and help the planet
  • ⏰ Save Time — Use carpool lanes, arrive refreshed
  • 👥 Build Community — Meet fellow Sun Devils and make connections
  • 🎓 Network — Expand your ASU network during your commute

Whether you're a driver looking to offset costs and meet people, or a rider seeking a reliable commute, ASU Carpool Connect makes it easy to find your perfect carpool match—all within the ASU community.

✨ How It Works

🚗 For Drivers

Post your ride details, set your schedule, and connect with riders. Earn money or just meet cool people while you commute.

🛣️ For Riders

Browse available rides, see driver details, and request a seat. Safe, affordable, and community-driven.

🔒 ASU Verified

All users must verify with an @asu.edu email. Only ride with fellow Sun Devils. Safety and trust first.
